About us
We manage a single, diversified portfolio across equities, bonds, commodities, and crypto, with the aim to build a low beta portfolio, that could harvest volatility and time value via option strategy.
Portfolio Investment
Asset allocation, Option, Valuation
The portfolio investment at Gordian Knot Capital is committed to building a portfolio across a wide range of asset types. Each asset class is paired with tailored options strategies to enhance performance under the supervision of the risk department to achieve sustainable investment.
Alternative Investment
Cryptocurrency, Short-swing trade
The Alternative Investments at Gordian Knot Capital is dedicated to exploring high-risk, high-reward opportunities beyond traditional asset classes. This department focuses on cryptocurrency, and short swing trades based on short-term signals, and leveraging financial release dates to capitalize on market movements.
Risk Management
Greeks-based risk decomposition, Monte Carlo Simulation
We introduce detailed risk profiles, incorporating metrics like the beta-adjusted delta exposure to the benchmark, Vega Edge PnL, and Value at Risk (VaR), to help clients understand the risk associated with their investments.
