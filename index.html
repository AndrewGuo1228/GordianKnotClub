What Our Club Does
As a student-run hedge fund, we aim to stimulate real-world asset management experience for UCSD students by employing option integrated investment strategies that balance risk and growth beyond textbook knowledges, empowering members with practical skills in risk assessment and investment.
Investment
Low market correlation, Steady returns, Time investing.
We diversify across various asset types including equities, bonds, REITs, commodities (oil, gold, natural gas), cryptocurrencies, and country-level macro investments (China, Japan). Each asset is paired with specific options strategies to optimize returns.
Risk Management
VaR, Sharpe ratio, Volatility
We introduce detailed risk profiles, incorporating metrics like the Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown, and Value at Risk (VaR), to help clients understand the risk associated with their investments. A significant challenge has been to treat assets and their corresponding options positions as a unified entity in these calculations.
Professional Knowledges
CFA, FRM, DCF, Competitions
We provide a structured curriculum covering portfolio theory, risk management, and professional certifications, with experienced members sharing their CFA and FRM insights. Practical lessons include guided asset management and strategic decision-making using models like the Gordon Growth Model and DCF. Additionally, we lead our team in participating in stock pitch, private equity, and quantitative competitions hosted by renowned institutions like UBS.
